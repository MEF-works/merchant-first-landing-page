The real problem

Processors do not have to warn you before your revenue stops.

Stripe, Square, PayPal, Shopify Payments, acquiring banks, gateways, and card networks all make risk decisions. Your business needs a plan for the day one of them says no.

01

Policy changes

Products that were tolerated yesterday can be rejected tomorrow. Hemp, kratom, 7-OH, supplements, firearms, and adjacent verticals all live under moving rules.

02

Payment workarounds

Manual Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, COD, money orders, and eCheck-only flows keep the lights on — but they hurt conversion and create operational drag.

03

No backup infrastructure

When a processor drops you and your only checkout dies, every customer becomes a support ticket and every hour becomes lost revenue.

FAQ

Questions merchants ask before they move.

Is this a payment processor?

No. MerchantFirst is a pre-qualification and routing front door for high-risk merchants. The actual placement depends on acquiring partners, underwriting, business details, and compliance fit.

Is approval guaranteed?

No. Nobody honest can guarantee approval before underwriting. The value is speed, clarity, better packaging, and identifying red flags before they waste your time.

What verticals do you work with?

Kratom, 7-OH style extract merchants, CBD/hemp, THCA, supplements, firearms, ammo/accessories, vape, and other regulated or policy-sensitive ecommerce categories.

What if we already process cards?

Good. That is the best time to build redundancy. The strongest merchants do not wait for a termination notice before they create fallback payment infrastructure.

Why does the wizard save progress?

High-risk submissions need details. Autosave lets you start now, come back later, and avoid losing the information that makes your file underwritable.
